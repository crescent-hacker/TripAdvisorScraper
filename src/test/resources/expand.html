“ Outstanding tour! Professional and efficient ”
Reviewed 3 days ago NEW

JD tours was thoroughly professional and helpful from the moment I contacted them. Booking a tour of Jordan, a country we'd never visited, from the other side of the world, without any knowledge of which company to use, can be a bit challenging. I emailed a couple of companies, and JD tours impressed me straight away. The very helpful Ashleigh assisted me in packing a very busy schedule (Amman, Petra, Dead Sea, Wadi Rum) into a four day itinerary, including meals, transfers and all the experiences we were hoping for, including my many changes and alterations.

From the moment we landed at Queen Alia airport, everything went smoothly. I was a little hesitant in handing over so much cash at the airport, so Ashleigh arranged for this to be done in the hotel instead.

An airport representative met us prior to customs, and helped us pass through customs and baggage claim, guiding us to a car, where we met our driver/guide/everything useful Wesam.

Wesam proved to be a wealth of knowledge about everything Jordan related, and was with us from the start of the trip right through until our departure. In fact, Wesam became a friend, not just a driver/guide. Wesam took us to the local restaurants we'd have never found by ourselves, and showed us some of the local foods, so we could get a taste of the country during our short stay.

Wesam was incredibly helpful, and flexible to allow us to stop and do things that interested us, re-working the itinerary as we went, to ensure we got the best possible experience. Some of the biggest highlights were last minute ideas Wesam came up with - like a tour of the fruit and vege market in Amman (something of an interest of mine), and finding us local shawarma (which would have been ten times the price if he hadn't bought it!).

Our brief itinerary was:
Day one- arrive in Amman - city tour.
Day two- travel to Petra - nigh tour of Petra (highly recommended)
Day three- Petra and drive to Wadi Rum - desert jeep tour
Day four- Dead Sea and drive to Amman for dinner.
Day five- depart.

Jordan is crazily expensive - I still can't figure out why - but as with anywhere in the world, you get what you pay for. JD tours delivered exactly what they said they would, and the experience was above and beyond that, as Wesam's personal attention made it a living experience, rather than a whistle-stop tour of the country. The organisation was flexible and professional (thanks Ashleigh), and JD tours helped make our brief four days absolutely perfect. Thank you.
